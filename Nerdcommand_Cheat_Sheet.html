⚔️EXECUTION (OPEN THE SPREAD)

  1. Sell to Open the lower strike (Delta ~0.15) → collect premium
  2. Buy to Open a strike $0.50 below → your insurance (caps loss)
  3. Net = Credit − Insurance cost = money in today
  4. If price stays out of danger → you keep the full credit
SOFI EX: Stock $8 → Sell $7 Put (Δ.15) / Buy $6.50 Put. Credit ≈ $20, Max risk ≈ $30, ~40% return if it holds.
